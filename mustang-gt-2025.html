2025 Mustang GT Review

Performance, design, and features of the latest Mustang GT.

Overview

The 2025 Mustang GT is a powerful American muscle car that continues to combine aggressive styling with modern technology. With a 5.0L V8 engine, it delivers thrilling acceleration and a pure driving experience.

Performance

0-60 mph in just 4.2 seconds. Adaptive suspension keeps it stable on corners while still delivering a comfortable ride. The exhaust note is aggressive yet refined.

Interior & Tech

Driver-focused cockpit with updated infotainment, premium materials, and optional digital instrument cluster. Apple CarPlay and Android Auto come standard.

Verdict

The 2025 Mustang GT is a perfect blend of classic muscle and modern tech, appealing to both enthusiasts and everyday drivers looking for excitement on the road.
