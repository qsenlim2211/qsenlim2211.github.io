2025 Mustang GT Review

The latest Mustang GT delivers a perfect blend of classic muscle with modern tech.

Engine & Performance

The 2025 Mustang GT comes with a 5.0L V8 producing 480–500hp depending on trim. Key upgrades for track enthusiasts include:

  • 6-speed manual or 10-speed automatic Tremec transmission with rev-matching (manual only)
  • Limited-slip rear differential (Torsen type) for improved traction in corners
  • Optional MagneRide adaptive suspension adjusting damping in real-time for comfort and performance
  • Launch control for optimal 0–60 mph in 4.2 seconds
  • Active exhaust valves for variable sound and performance tuning

Brakes & Handling

For spirited driving, Mustang GT offers upgraded brakes and handling tech:

  • Brembo 6-piston front and 4-piston rear calipers with vented discs (GT Premium Package)
  • Electronic stability control and line-lock feature for precise track starts
  • Front and rear sway bars tuned for both street comfort and track performance

Interior & Tech

Inside, the Mustang GT mixes modern tech with driver-focused design:

  • 12-inch customizable digital instrument cluster with performance gauges
  • Performance Pack options: Track Apps, G-meter, lap timer, and tire temp monitoring
  • Recaro sport seats with adjustable bolsters for lateral support during high-G cornering
  • SYNC 4 infotainment with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and optional B&O premium sound system

Exterior & Aerodynamics

  • Aggressive front splitter and rear spoiler improve high-speed stability
  • Widebody package available with flared fenders and staggered wheels for enhanced grip
  • Available carbon-fiber hood reduces weight and improves heat dissipation

Verdict for Enthusiasts

The 2025 Mustang GT is not just a street cruiser—it’s a track-capable muscle car. With proper tires, brake fluid, and minor tuning, it can keep pace with high-performance sports cars while maintaining everyday usability. Gear up, check fluids, and enjoy the raw V8 experience.
