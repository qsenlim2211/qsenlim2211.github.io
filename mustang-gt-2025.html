2025 Mustang GT Review

The ultimate muscle car experience with modern tech and track-ready performance.

Engine & Powertrain

  • 5.0L V8 Coyote engine producing 480–500hp depending on trim
  • 6-speed manual with rev-matching or 10-speed automatic
  • Limited-slip differential for optimal corner traction
  • Magnetic Ride Control (optional) for adaptive damping and comfort/performance balance
  • Launch Control feature for perfect 0–60 mph starts (approx. 4.2 sec)
Pro Tip: Use high-octane fuel and monitor oil temperatures for extended track sessions.

Braking & Suspension

  • Brembo 6-piston front and 4-piston rear calipers with slotted rotors (Premium/Performance Package)
  • Steel-braided brake lines to prevent fade
  • Front/rear sway bars for sharper cornering
  • Track-ready alignment and adjustable coilovers for enthusiasts

Interior & Tech

  • 12-inch customizable digital instrument cluster with performance gauges
  • Track Apps: G-force meter, lap timer, tire temperature monitoring
  • Recaro sport seats for lateral support during high-G cornering
  • SYNC 4 infotainment, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, optional B&O premium audio

Exterior & Aerodynamics

  • Aggressive front splitter and rear spoiler for stability at high speeds
  • Widebody package available with flared fenders for wider tires
  • Carbon-fiber hood for reduced weight and heat dissipation
  • Track-focused aero options: rear diffuser and front canards (optional)

Track & Street Tips

  • Warm tires and brakes before pushing hard on track
  • Use high-performance brake fluid (Motul RBF600) for repeated braking
  • Check tire pressures hot and cold, adjust for grip vs comfort
  • Monitor oil and coolant temps during track sessions
  • Consider lightweight wheels and sticky tires for serious track use
Pro Tip: For enthusiasts, pairing exhaust, ECU tuning, and suspension upgrades transforms the Mustang into a track monster while still being street-legal.
